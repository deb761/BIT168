Welcome to the SeaHorse Dive Club

The Boeing Employees SCUBA Club of Seattle, is a Boeing Recreation club dedicated to sharing our love of SCUBA diving, marine life, and our oceans with our fellow employees, families, and friends. Membership is open to all employees, retirees, their eligible family members, ad other eligible individuals (See Membership Information).

Join us on Facebook. We now have a Facebook page, where you can see the latest posts and pictures from fellow members, see where your friends are diving, find a buddy, see what everybody is up to, send messages to your fellow members, ask a question. It's easy to join, just visit the page and make a request to join our group. Any group member can sponsor you in.
